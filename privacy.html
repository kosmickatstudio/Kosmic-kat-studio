Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Kosmic Kat AI Studio ("we", "us", "the Studio") is operated by MOTIVAI PRIVATE LIMITED. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

  • Account info you provide when signing in (name, email) via Google, X, or GitHub
  • Content you create in the Studio (projects, characters, prompts, generated media) — stored locally in your browser
  • API keys you enter in Settings — stored only in your browser's local storage, never transmitted to our servers
  • Basic usage analytics (pages visited, features used) if analytics is enabled

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store your API keys on any server
  • We do not store your generated content on any server unless you explicitly export/upload it
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing

3. Third-Party Services

When you connect API keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, Seedance, ElevenLabs, etc.), your prompts and requests are sent directly from your browser to that provider, governed by their own privacy policy. We are not responsible for third-party data handling.

4. Payments

Payments are processed by Razorpay, Stripe, or Google Pay. We do not store your card or payment details — these are handled entirely by the payment processor.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your projects, settings, and preferences on your device. Clearing your browser data will remove this information.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your locally stored data anytime by clearing your browser's site data for this domain. To request account or data deletion related to authentication, contact us below.

7. Children's Privacy

This Studio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the Studio after changes constitutes acceptance.
