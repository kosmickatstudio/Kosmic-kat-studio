Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Kosmic Kat AI Studio uses minimal local storage technologies to function. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

1. What We Use

TypePurposeDuration
Local StorageSaves your projects, characters, shots, settings, and API keys on your deviceUntil you clear browser data
Session StorageTemporary UI state (active tab, panel open/closed)Until tab closes
Analytics Cookie (if enabled)Google Analytics — page views, feature usageUp to 2 years

2. Third-Party Cookies

If you sign in via Google, X, or GitHub, those providers may set their own authentication cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. Managing Cookies & Storage

You can clear all locally stored Studio data anytime via your browser settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → select this site.
